* Notes

These are notes by the author of the package for planning of new features, etc.

* Hexcrawl support

** Goal

- Ability to make hexcrawl rolls, according to my own rules, which take the following into account:
  - Target hex terrain
  - Day or night (has different effect depending on terrain)
  - Type of action (Travel, explore, visit, locate, forage, confront, rest)
- Outputs:
  - Time taken
  - Event - one of:
    - Encounter
    - Nothing
    - Find location
    - Find resource
    - Special

** Dashboard

- Switches:
  - Target hex terrain
  - Day or night
- Buttons
  - Make roll for
    - Travel
    - Explore
    - Visit
    - Locate
    - Forage
    - Confront
    - Rest
